Info

Aoife Ní Dhúnaigh is a User Experience Designer based in Dublin, Ireland.

She has worked on a variety of projects from traditional Desktop applications to Mobile and IoT solutions. Her expertise is in Research and Development, taking customer requirements and designing the software interactions required using Wireframes and Prototypes. Her unique perspective allows her to adapt any customer requirement to a usable and satisfying interaction.

She has a creative and thoughtful approach to discovering problems, influenced by her background in Music and the Arts, as well as a great team spirit from her background in sports.

Aoife started work in Web Development with handbag designer, Pauric Sweeney, in the summer of 2015. She was hired to the UA Prototyping Lab in SAP in September 2015, where she designed and built prototypes of solutions to common User Assistance problems with a small team of developers. She participated in site visits and customer interviews to gather requirements, analysed the findings and wireframed ideas for the team to develop.

In October 2016, Aoife was hired to SAP's Center of Excellence, developing and deploying internal projects with the Mobile / UX team, where she is presently working.
